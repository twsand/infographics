Wealth Inequality in America

Even with magic beans, you'll never catch up

What if your balloon business had zero expenses, perfect flying weather every day, and free crew—forever? How long would it take to match Elon Musk's wealth? The answer reveals why "billionaire" isn't just a bigger version of "successful."

Scale Comparison

Why we can't show you the gap

That's the point. The gap isn't just large. It's incomprehensible by design.

This isn't about you

When we talk about taxing billionaires, successful business owners often think we mean them. But even someone making millions every year—with every possible advantage—is still tens of thousands of years behind. That's not a gap you close with hard work. That's not a gap you close at all.
